Location History Visualizer

Welcome to Location History Visualizer, a tool for visualizing your collected Google Timeline data with heatmaps. Don't worry—all processing and visualization happens directly on your computer, so rest assured that nobody is able to access your Location History but you.

First download your data:

  • On Android: Phone settings → Location → Location Services → Timeline → Export Timeline data
  • On iOS: Google Maps app settings → Personal Content → Export Timeline Data

You should get a file named Timeline.json which you can upload here:
